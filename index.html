Midwifery legislations in Missouri

Like many other states in the country, legalization of midwifery practice in Missouri has been controversial and debatable. Missouri used to have a very strict regulation on midwifery practice since 1901. However, a 2007 midwifery law repealed the past regulations and legalized all major kinds of midwifery practice.
